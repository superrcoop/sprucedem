Some words about us

Spruce, defined as a tree of the genus picea. A picea of 35 species of coniferous evergreen trees in the family pinaceae,found in the nothern temperate and boreal regions of the earth. It has a distinctive conical shape and hanging cones,widely grown for timber ,pulp and Christmas trees. It is a versatile tree that is fast growing and proves dominant within its territory. Its wood is good at storing /conducting energy and it is seen as good to use when bargaining with earth elementals. With this extensive background, we the 'sprucedem' coined the term 'spruce' as the people that will represent such amazing tree as a symbolism of nobility , peace, endurance ,'weathering the storm',emitting energies of acceptance,grace and wisdom.From this we are determined to move forward into a profitable/nonprofitable organization to change the world.
