about us

Spruce, defined as a tree of the genus picea. A picea of 35 species of coniferous evergreen trees found in the nothern temperate and boreal regions of the earth. It has a distinctive conical shape and hanging cones,widely grown for timber and Christmas trees. It is a versatile tree that is fast growing and proves dominant within its territory. With that,'sprucedem' coined the term 'spruce' as the people that will represent such an amazing tree as a symbolism of nobility , peace, endurance ,'weathering the storm',emitting energies of grace and wisdom.
